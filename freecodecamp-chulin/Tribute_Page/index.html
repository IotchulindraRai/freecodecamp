Nelson Mandela

Biography

Nelson Rolihlahla Mandela was a South African anti-apartheid revolutionary, political leader, and philanthropist who served as President of South Africa from 1994 to 1999. He was the country's first black head of state and the first elected in a fully representative democratic election. Mandela's presidency marked the end of decades of institutionalized racial segregation, and he is widely regarded as a symbol of peace and reconciliation.
